Our Story

Born from the sun-soaked sands of America's #1 beach

Siesta Sizzlin' was born from a simple idea: capture the essence of Siesta Key Beach in a bottle. Our founder, a longtime resident of this paradise, wanted to share the vibrant flavors and laid-back lifestyle of our beautiful beach town with the world. Every bottle is crafted with locally-sourced ingredients when possible, and infused with the spirit of our crystal-clear waters and powder-white sand.
